WORLD TRIP

Overview

This is an experimental website I created, incorporating the concept of APIs for the first time to streamline the process of collecting data. The website features 100 maps from various countries categorized in three ways: Year, Location, and Scale. The central idea here is to blur the lines of geographical location, country size, and the digitization or abstraction of concrete maps. This serves as a statement against the centralization of power within a single state.
