Overview

Perhaps because we live in a different world from Marine life, people don't know much about them and thus call underwater world a mysterious place. Therefore, I want to design a book to introduce people to all creatures living underwater, endangered species, and the increasingly fragile Marine environment under the influence of human activities. After getting to know them, I believe it will be easier for people to empathize with marine life. This is a project to translate wikipedia content into a physical book. The term I chose was “Marine life”. All the images and text are from the original Wikipedia.
